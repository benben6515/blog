55 Common English Phrases For Daily Conversation

發表於 2023-07-19 分類於 英文筆記 Disqus：

This is a note from the following resourse.

ref: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EhRtGlnxTNc

  1. How to ask ‘How are you’
    1. How’ve you been? How'v ya been
    2. What’ve you been up to? What-af-you been up to
    3. How are thing?
    4. How’s it going?
    5. What’s up?
  2. How to respond to ‘How are you’ questions
    1. can’t complain
    2. pretty good
    3. never been better / could be better
    4. same old
  3. How to say ‘thank you’
    1. You’re a star!
    2. You’re the best!
    3. You’re an angel!
    4. I own you one.
    5. I appreciate it. I apprecia-dit
    6. That’ fantastic.
    7. That’s brilliant.
    8. That’s great.
    9. (respond) It’s (a/my) pleasure.
    10. (respond) No worries. / Don’t worry about it.
    11. (respond) Don’t mention it. / It’s nothing.
    12. (respond) You’re so welcome. / Happy to help.
  4. How to show agreement
    1. Absolutely!
    2. I totally agree with you.
    3. Tell me about it! (don’t need to tell)
    4. Exactly!
    5. You hit the nail on the head.
  5. How to express disagreement
    1. Not necessarily.
    2. I don’t see it that way.
    3. That’s interesting, but …
  6. How to respond to a change of plans
    1. No worries. / Don’t worry about it.
    2. These things happen. / Things happens.
    3. It can’t be helped.
    4. No biggie!
  7. How to respond to an invitation
    1. Sounds good!
    2. I’d love to!
    3. Sorry, I can’t.
    4. I won’t be able to make it.
    5. Unfortunately, I won’t be able to make it.
    6. I hate to miss it, but …
    7. I think I’ll pass.
  8. How to end a conversation
    1. I must make a move.
    2. I need ot get going.
    3. I better get to move on.
    4. I’ve taken up (enough / too much) of your time.
    5. I have another commitment.
  9. How to say goodbye
    1. See you later. / Later!
    2. Speak soon!
    3. Talk to you later.
    4. Stay in touch.
    5. Take care!
