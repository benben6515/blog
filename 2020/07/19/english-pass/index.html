口說英語通 | English All Pass

發表於 2020-07-19 分類於 英文筆記 Disqus：

國立教育廣播電臺

30個最常見與外國友人聊天的話題，訓練並提升日常生活英語的口說能力。

Please introduce yourself

Section 1:

  • As far as my hobbies go, I enjoy + Ving.
  • I’m into + N/Ving.

Talk about your family and how close you are

Section 2:

  • I have a big/small extended family with + N.
  • … is the oldest/youngest of + N.
  • We keep in touch over/through …

What do you usually eat? What are your eating habits?

Section 3:

  • … as many + C.N. as one can.
  • … as much + U.N. as possible.
  • I love to pig out on …
  • But overall, I would say …

Tell us about your favorite food.

Section 4:

  • My favorite + N + is …
  • too much … can be …
  • One thing I believe we can all agree on …
  • There is nothing better than …

Do you eat out a lot? Talk about some of your experince.

Section 5:

  • get used to + N/Ving.
  • Ving … is …
  • I would + V …

What is your favorite holiday?

Section 6:

  • My favorite + N. + is definitely …
  • You get … from …
  • It’s always fun + Ning …

Describe a trandition festival in your country.

Section 7:

  • has/have always been my favorite + N
  • Aside from + N/Ving
  • look forward to + N/Ving

Do you exercise? Talk about how often and what kind of exercise you do.

Section 8:

  • But personally for me, …
  • I like to + V
  • find the things that …
